Refund Policy

📅 Effective Date: June 2025 🏢 Publisher: Gravity Games 🔄 Last Updated: June 2025
⚠️
Important: All in-app purchases made through our games are processed by Google Play Billing. Refunds are primarily handled by Google Play. This policy outlines our position and how we can assist you.

01 Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all in-app purchases made in any game published by Gravity Games on the Google Play Store. Since all transactions are processed through Google Play Billing, Google's own refund policies also apply and take precedence.

Our games may offer optional in-app purchases such as virtual currency, premium content, power-ups, ad removal, and other digital items.

02 Google Play Refund Policy

Google Play offers a 48-hour refund window for most in-app purchases. Within this period, you may be eligible to request a refund directly through Google Play:

How to request a refund via Google Play:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions
  3. Tap Budget & history → find your purchase
  4. Tap the purchase → Request a refund

Alternatively, visit Google Play Help or contact Google Play Support directly.

03 Eligible & Non-Eligible Refund Situations

✓ Eligible for Refund

  • Accidental purchase, item unused
  • Duplicate charge due to technical error
  • Item not delivered to your account
  • Bug prevented access to purchased item
  • Unauthorised purchase by a minor

✕ Generally Not Refundable

  • Purchase over 48 hours ago, item used
  • Changed your mind after consuming item
  • No longer playing or uninstalled game
  • Prior refund for same purchase type
  • Does not meet Google Play criteria

04 Subscriptions

If any of our games offer subscription-based purchases:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play
  • Cancelling stops future billing but does not automatically generate a refund for the current billing period
  • Access to subscription content will continue until the end of the paid period
  • Refunds for subscriptions are evaluated on a case-by-case basis

05 Unauthorised Purchases by Minors

If a child has made unauthorised purchases in one of our games, we take these situations seriously. Please contact us as soon as possible at gravitygames@gmail.com with details of the purchases. We will work with you and Google Play to resolve the situation.

We strongly recommend enabling Google Play purchase authentication on your device to prevent unauthorised purchases. You can set this under Google Play → Settings → Authentication.

06 How to Request a Refund from Us

If you are unable to obtain a refund through Google Play and believe you qualify under our policy, please contact us with the following information:

  1. Your Google Play order number (found in your confirmation email)
  2. The name of the game and the item purchased
  3. The date and amount of the purchase
  4. The reason for your refund request
  5. Any relevant screenshots or evidence
Send your request to: gravitygames@gmail.com
We aim to respond to all refund enquiries within 3–5 business days. Please note that while we will do our best to assist, final refund decisions for Google Play purchases are ultimately made by Google.

07 Processing of Approved Refunds

If a refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed back to the original payment method used on Google Play
  • Processing time is typically 3–10 business days depending on your bank or payment provider
  • Any virtual currency or items granted by the purchase will be removed from your account upon refund approval

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our games after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

09 Contact Us

For refund requests or any questions about this policy, please reach out to us. We are happy to assist and will do our best to resolve any issues fairly and promptly.
